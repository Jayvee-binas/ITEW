Job Description

We are looking for enthusiastic Customer Service Representatives to join our growing team. The ideal candidate will provide excellent customer support and ensure customer satisfaction through professional communication.

Responsibilities:
  • Handle customer inquiries via phone, email, and chat
  • Process customer orders and transactions
  • Resolve customer complaints and concerns

  • Collaborate with other departments to resolve issues
Requirements:

  • Ability to work in shifts
  • Experience in customer service is a plus
Benefits:

  • Transportation allowance

Work Schedule:
