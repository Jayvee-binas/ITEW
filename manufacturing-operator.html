Full-time On-site Entry Level

Job Description

We are seeking reliable Manufacturing Operators to join our production team. The ideal candidates will operate machinery and equipment in our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility.

Responsibilities:
  • Operate production machinery and equipment
  • Follow standard operating procedures
  • Monitor product quality
  • Maintain clean and organized work area
  • Report equipment issues to supervisors
  • Complete production logs accurately
  • Follow safety guidelines and procedures
Requirements:
  • High School Graduate or Vocational Course
  • Willing to work in shifts
  • Basic mathematical skills
  • Good eye-hand coordination
  • Can understand written and verbal instructions
  • Manufacturing experience is a plus
Benefits:
  • Above minimum wage compensation
  • Night differential pay
  • Overtime opportunities
  • HMO coverage
  • 13th month pay
  • Transportation allowance
  • Free uniforms
  • Training opportunities
Work Schedule:
  • 3 rotating shifts available
  • 6 days per week
  • 8-hour shifts
  • Regular overtime available
