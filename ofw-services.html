OFW Services

Supporting our Overseas Filipino Workers and their families

Our Services for OFWs

Pre-Departure Services

  • ✓ Pre-Departure Orientation Seminar (PDOS)

    Comprehensive orientation about working abroad, cultural adaptation, and rights awareness.

  • ✓ Document Processing Assistance

    Help with passport, visa, and other required documentation.

  • ✓ Contract Review

    Legal assistance in reviewing employment contracts.

Family Support Services

  • ✓ Financial Management Seminars

    Training on budgeting and investment for OFW families.

  • ✓ Educational Support

    Scholarship programs for OFW dependents.

  • ✓ Counseling Services

    Professional support for families dealing with separation.

Reintegration Programs

  • ✓ Business Development

    Support for OFWs planning to start businesses in Cabuyao.

  • ✓ Local Employment

    Job matching services for returning OFWs.

  • ✓ Skills Training

    Vocational and technical training programs.
