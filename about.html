About PESO Cabuyao

Empowering the workforce of Cabuyao City

Our Mission

The Public Employment Service Office (PESO) of Cabuyao City is committed to providing comprehensive employment facilitation services to job seekers and employers, promoting local economic development through effective labor market interventions.

Our Vision

To be the premier employment facilitation center in the region, connecting qualified job seekers with meaningful employment opportunities while supporting the growth of local businesses.

What We Do

  • ✓ Facilitate employment opportunities for local job seekers
  • ✓ Provide career counseling and guidance
  • ✓ Organize job fairs and employment programs
  • ✓ Support local businesses in their recruitment needs
  • ✓ Implement various DOLE programs and services
