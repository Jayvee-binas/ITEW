Production Supervisor

Manufacturing Excellence Inc.

Full-time On-site Experienced

Job Description

We are seeking an experienced Production Supervisor to oversee our manufacturing operations. The ideal candidate will ensure efficient production processes while maintaining quality and safety standards.

Responsibilities:
  • Supervise and coordinate production activities
  • Monitor production schedules and workflows
  • Ensure quality control standards are met
  • Manage and train production staff
  • Maintain safety protocols and procedures
  • Optimize production efficiency and reduce waste
  • Coordinate with maintenance team for equipment upkeep
  • Prepare and submit production reports
Requirements:
  • Bachelor's degree in Engineering or related field
  • 5+ years of manufacturing experience
  • 3+ years of supervisory experience
  • Strong knowledge of production processes and quality control
  • Excellent leadership and communication skills
  • Proficient in MS Office and production management software
  • Experience with lean manufacturing principles
Benefits:
  • Competitive salary package
  • Performance bonuses
  • Health insurance (HMO)
  • 13th month pay
  • Transportation allowance
  • Meal allowance
  • Professional development opportunities
Work Schedule:
  • Monday to Friday
  • 8-hour shifts
  • Rotating schedule (Morning/Afternoon/Night)
